Our Beliefs and Goals

We as a council, hold dear very fundamental goals on how the server should be structured, and how the community should grow.

  • A healthy community - We believe that everyone should be able to have fun and roleplay their characters in Project RAPTURE, so long as they don't break any Lore, or our Roleplay Etiquette.
  • Joint management - The Council exists fundmentally, to combine the heads of multiple people on a multi-dimensional universe. With multiple people, power trips and rampages are less likely, and we can collaborate on projects.
  • Autonomous roleplay - We of the Council believe that eventually, we won't have to stimulate roleplay for it to exist, and that the community won't need us to forward roleplay. We take on volunteer staff and get new community members everyday to strive towards this.
  • A fair roleplay enviroment - We believe that no character should be inheritly 'better' than another. Every character can achieve whatever they desire as long as it fits the lore, and is roleplayed out over time. Mary Sueing, Meta Gaming, etc, are strictly banned.
  • An interactive world - We believe that events and geopolitics is the way forward, in order to keep things fresh. Channel 17, Wasteland News, attacks on Antioch, etc, are how we try and give to the players, besides expanding on the lore. Quests are made to both further a storyline, and to stimulate the community.
