Our Community

Our first sixty members have been such an amazing treat to have around. Here are some of the things we want to do to encourage our community's growth, and engagement

Community Elections

Community-elected Game Master positions. We want our community to thrive, and if we need another Game Master, you get to vote who it is, to ensure the best quality.

Staff Applications

All our moderators will be accepted on a application basis. We want to make our community fun and exciting, and we will employ Moderators to help us do this.

Community Input

Community Input helps us shape our Lore and Server to best fit our Community's needs. Here, if you feel there is something missing, let us know!
