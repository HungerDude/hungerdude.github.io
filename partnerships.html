Our Partnerships

Partnerships allow us to join together with other servers, and bolster each other's member count. We all rise together this way.

HALO: NEW MOON

ONI has recruited and formed a specialized unit to carry out highly secretive ops. They serve aboard a ship that doesn't exist in a fleet that doesn't exist and do things that never happen. They test prototype weapons and fight against the deadliest of foes, and in return live in isolated luxury among close to 2000 or so other personnel around the entire fleet. They'll go to exotic and deadly locations and risk their lives for the good of the UNSC.

We start with Operation New Moon. The members of this fleet, known simply as Task Force Slate, must infiltrate a Forerunner shield world and retrieve riches that could turn the tide of the war, yet the problem is, this world and it's surrounding worlds are warring, and haven't seen anything outside of their systems for 200 years.

DISBOARD DISCORD

The MBDE invites you to join us in our adventures. With the 4 branches of the British Military, will you join the Army? And fight for control of the battlefield. Or are you a pilot? And join the Air Force, fighting for air supremacy and flying with extreme grace as you cover our boys on the ground. Or are you a sailor? Will you join the navy and work as a team to make sure our Vessels run smoothly, or are you going to be the next Captain? Commanding your ship to fend off various Aquatic Borne enemies and cover both our boys in the skies and on the ground. The Special Forces you say? You want to become an elite soldier, infiltrating buildings and sweeping the premises for terrorist fighters? Or will you join the MI6, and not only protect the Role Play, but the server on a whole? Joining our Moderation Team to bring order and justice to Britain?

We have:

-Role Play events.
-Many Bots at your disposal.
-Air Force, Army, Navy, UK Special Forces and the MI6.
-Game nights.
-Every rank in the 5 services you could need.
- A football league for you to participate in.
- Medals you can earn in and out of RP!

The choice is yours, maybe you'll even become the next Field Marshal of the British Army? Or the next Admiral of the Fleet of the Royal Navy. Who knows? Only you can choose your path.
